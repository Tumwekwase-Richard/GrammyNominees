About Us

We are grammy nominee records a music website
It is owned by Diamond Pivot a music promoter on social media
He is one of the music promoters in mbarara Uganda
He started this job in 2023 aiming at promoting music, organising concerts and awards per year

What we offer

This website is based on providing music both videos and audios to someone who access it
It is owned by Grammy nominee records and on it you can search for any song,and any artist
Then there are also options of playing the song and download it if you want

Our mission

This is aimed at making people access music at any place and any time of their wish
It is also aimed at promotion of music and the artistes of those music for recognition
To reward those artistes that have been the best in every particular year and producing nomination lists per each category
